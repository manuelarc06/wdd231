Project Subject: Swimming Club

Site Name

Blue Wave Swimming Club

I choose this name because it represents the connection to water and swimming while conveying energy, movement, and professionalism. It is easy to remember and suitable for a sports club.

Optional domain: bluewaveswimclub.com

Site Purpose

This website serves to inform potential members about the swimming club's activities, training programs, and membership options. It promotes the benefits of swimming while providing a professional online presence for the club.

Scenarios

  • Can I try a free trial session before joining?
  • What are the benefits of joining this swimming club?
  • How can I become a member of the club?

Color Scheme

Blue (#1e90ff): Used for headings, header background, and borders.

Yellow (#ffd700): Used for accents, section highlights, and visual emphasis.

Typography

Noto Serif, serif: Used for all text, including headings and body, ensuring readability and a cohesive, professional look.
